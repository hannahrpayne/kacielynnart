Kacie is a Utah based artist working in several mediums. She has been making art passionately for as long as she remembers. From rug design, digital art, character design, painting and more. Kacie pulls inspiration from fantasy stories, with a love of illustrating and painting swords, knights, eyes, crows, and various other subject matter. She is passionate about illustrative storytelling, currently working on her fantasy adventure comic The Crow Tales.
